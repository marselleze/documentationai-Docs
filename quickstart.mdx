## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- An active email address for account verification
- Access to `https://dashboard.example.com` (the Расписание КГУ dashboard)

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and log in to Расписание КГУ.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and fill in your details:
    
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Password (minimum 8 characters)
    
    Agree to the terms and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Расписание КГУ. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your email and password, then click **Log In**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Timetable

Build a basic weekly schedule for a class group.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Schedule" icon="plus">
    Click **New Schedule** in the sidebar. Enter:
    
    - Schedule name: `Math 101 - Week 1`
    - Group: `CS-101`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Events" icon="clock">
    Drag events from the toolbar or use quick add:
    
    | Time Slot | Subject     | Room | Instructor |
    |-----------|-------------|------|------------|
    | 09:00-10:30 | Algebra    | A-101| Dr. Smith |
    | 11:00-12:30 | Calculus   | B-202| Prof. Lee |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Share" icon="save">
    Click **Save**. Share via link or embed code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop" icon="monitor">
    Use drag-and-drop for easy event placement.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile" icon="smartphone">
    Tap to add events and pinch to zoom timeline.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Here is an example embed code for your schedule:

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,HTML">
  ```javascript
  // Embed in your site
  const embed = document.createElement('iframe');
  embed.src = 'https://dashboard.example.com/embed/math101?token=YOUR_TOKEN';
  embed.width = 800;
  embed.height = 600;
  document.body.appendChild(embed);
  ```
  ```html
  <!-- Simple iframe embed -->
  <iframe
    src="https://dashboard.example.com/embed/math101?token=YOUR_TOKEN"
    width="800"
    height="600"
    frameborder="0">
  </iframe>
  ```
</CodeGroup>
